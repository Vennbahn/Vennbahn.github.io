One short story

I know, nobody listened to me. But what you should know is, that I am the smartest person in the world.

Just kidding!

But I want to tell you the following story. It's a real one. It did happen.

I don't want to miss school!

It was a tuesday morning when Frank ran out of his house. He was late, but didn't want to miss school. so he jumped on his bike, drove onto the main street and rode downtown. He wanted to cross the main street but he had no time to wait for the traffic lights to go green. He didn't cross.

The next traffic lights he reached were green. Hurrying Frank crossed the street faster then normal. But a red car came and crossed his way and hit him from the left. Now, at this street, there is no Frank anymore, but a cross.
